Conversational Intelligence

To orchestrate the mood that brings about the space of Possibilities, that is the role of leadership

Fernando Flores, Chilean-born, engineer, thinker and one of the fathers of ontological coaching, through his doing and his challenging.

In a VUCA world we need working environments that are radically collaborative and agile. This is what Conscious Communication contributes to.

We understand the culture of an organization hinges on the relationships that are woven through the conversations we have and the ones we fail to have. And relationships are the drivers for results.

As learning architects, we blend awareness of impact, conversational intelligence, neurosciences, emotional and social management so you can lead through meaningful and effective 360 conversations.

What can the team do after the learning experience?

  • Hone leadership competencies for a VUCA world
  • Gain insights into what happens in our brains when we hold effective conversations
  • Listen beyond words and have conversations for results
  • Choose and design what kind of conversation you wish to have
  • Generate a systemic “WE” that enables facing challenges collaboratively
  • Understand and manage emotions for results

Storytelling - Powerful stories

I see society as a network of stories; it is not just a network of economic and emotional exchanges. Rather it is a web of (interwoven) stories

Ricardo Piglia, Italian writer

We understand history, what has happened, and we imagine futures through stories we listen to, we tell ourselves and we give life to. Stories that guide our actions and our results.

Do you want to improve on your personal storytelling, your corporate storytelling?

We offer you

  • Storytelling workshops
  • One-to-one coaching sessions to build your story and/or your personal brand
  • Team storytelling: if the story involves different storytellers, we can blend individual and team activities
  • Editing for impact: you generate your story, we spruce it up, jazz it up, prune it for effect, make it over and “Wow” it with you and for you to suit your audience.

CCS Coaching Communication Skills – Communication Coaching

The limits of my language are the limits of my world

Ludwig Wittgenstein

You know your language competencies in English as a non-native speaker enable you to communicate with a certain solvency, and yet you want agile, impactful and successful conversations.

Credentialed coaches, savvy in multicultural environments and with proven tools in language learning will walk you towards generating the spin and the impact you want.

A communication coach can help you

  • Design your public image and your strategy
  • Build on your strengths as communicator
  • Generate relationships and results with different cultures
  • Gain awareness of emotional state suitable for each given interaction

About Clara Beleiro

Clara is a bilingual executive coach, PCC, ICF Professional Certified Coach. Since the beginning of her career in corporate environments she accompanies C-suite executives, entrepreneurs, business people and leaders to communicate effectively and get things done.

She is an expert in personal branding (Reach360®).

She mentors teams for coaches in organizational environments and mentors coaches-to-be in an ICF accredited coaching school. She’s a passionate learner, fascinated by how we relate to each other through conversations and design futures together.

She is the founder and Chief Learning Architect at CBnetwork Aprendizajes en Comunicación. She is an executive, team and organizational coach and crafts training programs in conscious communication and impactful leadership for various industries such as pharma, oil, finance, ag business, automotive, insurance and port operations.

She’s the architect of the 4 C’s ®, a conscious leadership and organizational transformation program based on Connecting, Communicating, Confronting and Co-creating.

She has co-crafted a mentoring program for coaches called E4C.
